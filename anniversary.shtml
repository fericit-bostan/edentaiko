15th Anniversary Performance

It has been a 15 years since I started teaching taiko, and 5 years since the 10 year anniversary concert. It went really well and we had great fun. Everyone worked hard and two groups from Japan made it an even greater event.

This year, our 15th year conincides with the one year anniversary of Earthquake/Tsunami disaster in Japan. There are still lot of issues faced by the people victimized by the disaster.

We will have the following dance groups collaborating with us for this performance.

Lyric Dance and Vocal Ensemble
East Bay
Valverde Peruvian Dance
San Francisco
Chhandam Indian Youth Company
San Francisco
Jubilee American Dance
Berkeley
Eden Aoba Taiko Dance
Hayward / San Lorenzo
